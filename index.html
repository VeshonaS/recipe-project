Mac n Cheese

This kind mac and cheese, is different from the way other make it. Mac n Cheese is a very popular all over the nation, sometimes made baked or sometimes with breadcrumbs on top, it's loved by kids and adults nationwide. This recipe isn't baked,or has bread crumbs, it's cooked on the stove and it makes it good and cheesey

Ingredients

  • American cheese
  • Cheddar Cheese
  • Muenster cheese
  • Milk
  • Salt
  • *secret ingredient*
  • pepper
  • water
  • elbow pasta noodles
  • butter

Procedure

  1. Add salt to a pot of water, until water is almost white. Wait until the water has reached it's boiling point, if not pasta could get water logged (for faster process proceed to step 4 while pasta is cooking).
  2. Once water is boiling, add the elbow pasta (using a strainer before or after pasta is cooked is optional). Wait, until pasta is a little over al dente.
  3. Strain pasta, and add about 1 tbsp of butter (preferably unsalted, pasta aready has salt (not trying to kill ourselves )).
  4. In a pot (to save dishwashing you could use the same pot you cooked the pasta in.) add 2 cups of milk, wait until hot but not too long or the milk with develop film(skin) over it.
  5. Once the milk is hot add cheese (and the secret ingrediant) and let it melt, keep adding cheese until it's reached a cream thick consistency.
  6. Once the cheese is melted and thick, quicky add the strained pasta into the cheese pot and stir until all pasta is covered in cheese.
  7. Now the Mac n Cheese is ready to serve.
